ABOUT NTPC SCHOOL OF BUSINESS

It recognizes the centrality of energy for inclusive growth driven through interplay of regulations, market instruments and institutional mechanisms. An equally important opportunity is to respond to India’s call to create and consolidate her niche in related areas of management in the community of nations globally. This includes energy and resource modelling, variety of externalities, economics, trade, environmental management, technology, equity, justice, law, management and communication systems and community interfaces duly building capacities of all related stakeholders. The course embeds well defined and emerging learning on these and related facets of management relevant at the regional, national, and global levels. It helps emerging professionals with a 360 degree perspective, essential to capture career opportunities and importantly fulfil aspirations of businesses with the country, nations and humanity as a whole.

WHY PGDM IN ENERGY MANAGEMENT

CURRICULUM & PEDAGOGY

The curriculum is designed by experienced faculty members and experts from various reputed institutions, and veterans from leading global energy organizations.

The pedagogy includes lectures, case studies, group projects, discussions, live research projects, simulation and management games. Elective courses are offered based on the interest, areas of specialization and industry requirements.

KEY BENEFITS OF THE PROGRAMME

The programme encourages discussion using Case based pedagogy, interactive guest lecture classroom to prepare students for critical analysis and application of skill to real business situations.

Students gain advantages from learning theoretical and practical aspects of energy management from Professors of IIMA, NSB and energy sector veterans.

Multiple opportunities are made available for students that encourage their growth through experiential learning. Such activities include internships, field visits, research projects, and guest lectures.
